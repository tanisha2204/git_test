Recipe of rajma chawal

Here is the video tutorial

Description

Rajma chawal is a classic North India comfort food made from rajma and chawal.It is loved for its wholesome,erthy flavors and is a staple in many North Indian households

Ingredients

  • rajma
  • tomatoes
  • turmeric
  • garam masala
  • green chillies
  • garlic
  • ginger
  • oil or ghee
  • rice
  • salt
  • onion

    • Steps
    • 2.Heat oil and add the cumin seeds. When they sizzle and begin to change colour, add the onions. When they turn pink, add the powdered cinnamon, ginger, and green chillies. Fry for 2-3 mins. 3.Then add the coriander powder, amchur or tamarind, and turmeric. Mix well and cook for another 3-4 mins. My onion-masala mixture is red because I used red chilli powder instead of green chillies. 4.Add the cooked rajma along with the water used to cook it in and the sliced tomato. Mix well and let simmer for 5 mins. 5.When the gravy thickens a bit, add the chaat masala and salt. Mix well, bring to boil again and turn off heat. 6.Add cooked rice and mix gently. 7.Garnish with chopped coriander leaves (cilantro) and serve warm with some plain yogurt.
