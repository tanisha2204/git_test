Pav Bhaji Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (potatoes, peas, carrots, bell peppers)
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 1 green chili, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 4 tablespoons pav bhaji masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 6 pav (bread rolls)
  • Fresh coriander, chopped (for garnish)
  • Lemon wedges (for serving)

Instructions:

  1. Boil the mixed vegetables until soft, then mash them and set aside.
  2. In a pan, heat 2 tablespoons of butter. Add chopped onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  3. Add green chili and ginger-garlic paste. Cook for a couple of minutes.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they become soft.
  5. Stir in the pav bhaji masala and salt, and cook for a few minutes.
  6. Add the mashed vegetables and mix well. Cook for another 5-10 minutes, adding water for desired consistency.
  7. In another pan, heat 2 tablespoons of butter and toast the pav until golden brown.
  8. Serve the bhaji hot, garnished with coriander, alongside the buttered pav and lemon wedges.
