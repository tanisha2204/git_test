Simple Sandwich Recipe

Ingredients:
  • 2 slices of bread (whole grain, white, or your choice)
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise or butter
  • 1 leaf of lettuce
  • 2 slices of tomato
  • 2 slices of cheese (cheddar, mozzarella, or your choice)
  • 2-3 slices of deli meat (ham, turkey, or chicken)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: pickles, onions, or any other toppings you like

Instructions:

  1. Spread mayonnaise or butter on one side of each slice of bread.
  2. Place the lettuce leaf on one slice of bread, mayonnaise side up.
  3. Add the tomato slices on top of the lettuce.
  4. Layer the cheese slices over the tomatoes.
  5. Top with the deli meat, followed by any additional toppings you like.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Place the second slice of bread on top, mayonnaise side down.
  8. Cut the sandwich in half and serve immediately.
